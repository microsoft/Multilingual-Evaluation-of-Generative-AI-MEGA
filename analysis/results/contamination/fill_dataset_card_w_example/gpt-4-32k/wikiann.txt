dataset_summary: WikiANN is a multilingual named entity recognition dataset consisting of over 100,000 annotated sentences for 40 languages. The dataset is derived from Wikipedia articles, and the annotations are automatically generated by matching Wikipedia links with corresponding Wikidata items. The goal is to predict the named entity tags (e.g., person, location, organization) for each token in a given sentence.
